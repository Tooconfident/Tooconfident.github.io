CSS Positioning

position:static

Default position. Elements fall in the normal flow of the page.

position:fixed**

Ignores scrolling. Taken out of flow of page. Elements positioned in relation to the browser.

position:absolute**

Parent must be fixed, absolute, or relative. Taken out of flow of page. Elements positioned in relation to its parent element.

position:relative**

Used often. Great companion to absolute. Looks where element would have been and shifts it from original spot, but reserves the original space.

**set with top/left/bottom/right

Inline elements

Use: margin-left, margin-right, padding

Found inside content: span, em, strong, etc.

Float

Pulls element out of flow of page and moves it as far right or left as possible

Great for columns, or wrapping text around image. Text will go around objects with float property.
